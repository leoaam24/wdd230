In Davao City, the gender distribution is skewed towards males, with 60% of the population being male and 40% being female. When considering age groups, the largest proportion of the population falls within the 15 to 34 years old category, accounting for 37.71% of the population. This is followed by the 14 years old and below group at 30.38%, the 35 to 54 years old group at 21.72%, the 55 to 74 years old group at 8.97%, and finally, those above 75 years old at 1.23%.

Davao City has achieved a commendable milestone in its economic progress, with an annual regular revenue of 6,283,803,912.64 PHP as of 2015. This substantial revenue indicates the city's strong financial management and successful implementation of revenue-generating initiatives. It showcases Davao City's ability to generate resources and invest in various sectors, such as infrastructure, education, healthcare, and social services, for the benefit and well-being of its residents. The significant revenue figure reflects a prosperous and thriving city that is committed to providing quality services and improving the overall standard of living for its inhabitants.

Davao City boasts an impressive array of tourist destinations, with over 100 captivating sites to explore. This abundance of attractions reflects the city's rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and diverse offerings for visitors. From pristine beaches and lush mountains to historical landmarks and vibrant markets, Davao City provides a wide range of experiences for tourists. The presence of such a vast number of tourist destinations highlights the city's commitment to promoting tourism, providing ample opportunities for leisure, adventure, and cultural immersion. With its extensive selection of attractions, Davao City is positioned as a vibrant and enticing destination for both local and international travelers seeking memorable experiences and unforgettable adventures.

Davao City stands as a shining example of safety and security, as it has consistently earned its reputation as one of the safest cities in the Philippines. In 2022, the city's remarkable achievement was underscored when it claimed the top spot out of 163 cities in terms of peace and order. This remarkable feat marks the 19th consecutive year that Davao City has been recognized for its commitment to maintaining a safe environment for its residents and visitors alike. Such consistent rankings demonstrate the city's effective law enforcement, proactive governance, and community involvement in ensuring the well-being and protection of its populace. Davao City's continued emphasis on safety contributes to its desirability as a place to live, work, and explore, instilling a sense of confidence and peace of mind among its inhabitants and fostering a thriving and harmonious community.

Davao City boasts an exceptionally high literacy rate, with over 95% of its population proficient in reading and writing. This achievement reflects the city's commitment to education and the empowerment of its residents. By placing a strong emphasis on literacy, Davao City creates a foundation for intellectual growth, personal development, and socioeconomic advancement. The high literacy rate opens doors to enhanced opportunities for individuals, including access to higher education, better job prospects, and increased engagement in civic and cultural activities. This remarkable accomplishment showcases Davao City's dedication to fostering a knowledgeable and informed community, creating a positive environment for lifelong learning and continued progress.
