Target Audience

Who: CEOs, Business Owners, and Stakeholders who want to be a part of a community engaged in and enthusiastic about growing their businesses and networks.

Age: Between 18 to 80 years old

Education: Atleast High School graduate

Income: Not less than PHP 3,000,000

Goal: To be part of a very selected group and be updated about activities and event that will be held.

Personas

Henry Dela Cruz

Occupation: Business Owner/Co-Founder of a food restaurant

Demographics:

  • Married
  • Business Degree College

Goal and Motivation:

Henry is looking to expand his customer base and increase his restaurant's visibility in the local community. Scenarios

  • What are the benefits of joining a chamber of commerce, and how can they help me grow my business?
  • How can I get involved in the chamber's initiatives and contribute to the local community?
  • How can I leverage the chamber's network to connect with other professionals in my industry and generate leads for my business?
  • What kind of events and resources does the chamber offer, and how can I make the most of them?
  • How can the chamber help me navigate local regulations and policies that may impact my business, and how can I get involved in advocacy efforts to support my industry?
