Site Purpose

The website of Davao City Chamber of Commerce and Industry Inc. aims to provide easy access to services, resources, and programs for businesses in Davao City, the Philippines, and beyond, supporting grassroots entrepreneurship, promoting international trade, and advocating for the economic interests of the region. The site navigation will enable users to quickly find relevant information on chamber development, business innovation, operating efficiency, and more, helping them to enhance their competitiveness and growth.
